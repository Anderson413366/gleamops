# Quickstart — Get Productive in 10 Minutes

> You're new to GleamOps. This page gets you oriented fast.

---

## What Is GleamOps?

GleamOps is an ERP for commercial cleaning companies.
It replaces spreadsheets for scheduling, client management, bidding, inventory, and operations.

**One sentence:** Everything your cleaning business needs, in one place.

---

## Step 1: Log In

1. Go to [gleamops.vercel.app](https://gleamops.vercel.app).
2. Enter your email and password.
3. Click **Sign In**.
4. You'll land on the **Home** dashboard.

> **If** you can't log in **→** check your email for an invitation link. Ask your admin to verify your account exists.

---

## Step 2: Understand the Sidebar

The left sidebar is your navigation. It has 13 modules:

| Icon | Module | What It's For |
|------|--------|--------------|
| Home | **Home** | Your dashboard. KPIs, alerts, quick links. |
| Calendar | **Schedule** | Employee schedules, work orders, planning boards |
| Clipboard | **Jobs** | Service plans, tickets, inspections, time tracking |
| Building | **Clients** | Client companies, their sites, and contacts |
| Trending | **Pipeline** | Sales: prospects, opportunities, bids, proposals |
| Layers | **Catalog** | Task library and service definitions |
| Users | **Team** | Staff, positions, attendance, payroll, HR |
| Package | **Inventory** | Supplies, kits, counts, orders |
| Wrench | **Equipment** | Equipment, keys, vehicles, maintenance |
| Shield | **Safety** | Certifications, training, incidents |
| Chart | **Reports** | Dashboards for ops, sales, workforce, inventory |
| Gear | **Settings** | Lookups, geofences, rules, data import |
| Clock | **Shifts & Time** | Clock in/out, timesheets (for field staff) |

Each module expands to show sub-tabs when you click it.

---

## Step 3: Your First 5 Actions

### Action 1: View Your Clients
1. Click **Clients** in the sidebar.
2. You see a list of all clients.
3. Click any client name to see their detail page.

### Action 2: Check Today's Schedule
1. Click **Schedule** in the sidebar.
2. The **Employee Schedule** grid shows this week's shifts.
3. Use the arrows to navigate weeks.

### Action 3: Search for Anything
1. Press `Cmd+K` (Mac) or `Ctrl+K` (Windows).
2. Type what you're looking for.
3. Click a result to jump there.

### Action 4: Create a New Client
1. Go to **Clients**.
2. Click the **+ New Client** button (top left).
3. Fill in the name and status.
4. Click **Create Client**.

### Action 5: View a Staff Member
1. Click **Team** in the sidebar.
2. Click **Staff** tab.
3. Click any staff name to see their profile.

> **Stop Point:** You now know how to navigate, search, and find key data. Take a break if you need one.

---

## Step 4: Understand Views

Most modules have three view options:

| View | What It Shows |
|------|--------------|
| **List** (default) | Table with sortable columns |
| **Card** | Grid of cards with avatar, name, status |
| **Export** | Download filtered data as CSV |

Toggle between List and Card using the view buttons (top right of any table).

---

## Step 5: Understand Status Chips

Above every table, you'll see status filter chips:

```
[ACTIVE] [INACTIVE] [ON_HOLD] [all]
```

- Click a chip to filter the list.
- The number badge shows how many items have that status.
- Click **all** to see everything.
- The default filter is usually **ACTIVE**.

> **If** a table looks empty **→** click **all** to remove the status filter. Your data may have a different status.
